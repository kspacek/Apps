CupWeigh

Privacy Policy · Effective April 9, 2026

The short version: CupWeigh does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. The app works entirely offline on your device.

Data Collection

CupWeigh does not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account, login, or any form of registration. No names, email addresses, or other identifiers are ever requested or stored.

Usage Data

CupWeigh does not collect analytics, usage statistics, crash reports, or any other telemetry data. The app does not contain any third-party tracking or analytics SDKs.

Network Access

CupWeigh operates entirely offline. The app does not connect to the internet and does not send or receive any data over a network. All ingredient data and conversion calculations are performed locally on your device.

Data Storage

Any preferences or saved conversions are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any server or third party. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

In-App Purchases

CupWeigh offers optional tip jar purchases ("Buy me a coffee"). These are processed securely through Apple's App Store. UDT s.r.o. does not collect or store any payment information — all transactions are handled entirely by Apple.

Third-Party Services

CupWeigh does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms. There are no ads in the app.

Children's Privacy

CupWeigh does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Since the app collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.

Údaje o používání

CupWeigh neshromažďuje analytická data, statistiky používání, hlášení o pádech ani žádná jiná telemetrická data. Aplikace neobsahuje žádné sledovací ani analytické SDK třetích stran.

Přístup k síti

CupWeigh funguje zcela offline. Aplikace se nepřipojuje k internetu a neodesílá ani nepřijímá žádná data přes síť. Veškerá data o surovinách a výpočty převodů probíhají lokálně na vašem zařízení.

Změny těchto zásad
